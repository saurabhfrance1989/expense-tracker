Application Documentation

Welcome to the Expense Tracker application documentation.

Overview

This application helps you track your monthly income and expenses.

Features

  • Track Monthly Income
  • Log Various Expense Categories
  • View Summary of Income, Expenses, and Balance
  • Download Expense Data as CSV
  • User Registration and Login

How to Use

1. Registration and Login

If you are a new user, please register an account using the "Register here" link on the login page. Once registered, you can log in with your credentials.

2. Tracking Income

On the main page (index.html), enter your total monthly income in the "Monthly Income" section.

3. Logging Expenses

Enter amounts for each predefined expense category in the "Monthly Expenses" section. The summary will update automatically.

4. Viewing Summary

The summary cards show your total income, total expenses, and the remaining balance.

5. Downloading Data

Click the "Download Expenses as CSV" button to save your income and expense data locally.

6. Logging Out

Click the "Logout" button in the header to end your session.
