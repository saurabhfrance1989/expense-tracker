Overview of the Expense Tracker

This application helps you track your monthly income and expenses with a user-friendly dashboard interface. All data is stored locally in your browser, specific to your user account.

Key Features

  • User Registration and Login (data is user-specific).
  • Set and Update Monthly Income.
  • Add New Expenses: Record description, amount, category, and date.
  • View Expenses: Displayed in a sortable and filterable table.
  • Edit and Delete individual expense entries.
  • Filter Expenses: By category and by month.
  • Financial Summary: View total income, total expenses, and remaining balance.
  • Download Expense Data: Export your expenses as a CSV file.
  • Reset User Data: Option to clear all your income and expense data.
  • Responsive Design: Works on desktop, tablets, and mobile devices.

How to Use

1. Registration and Login

If you are a new user, navigate to the Login Page and click "Register here". Fill in your desired username and password. Once registered, you can log in with your credentials.

2. Setting Your Income

Once logged in, you'll land on the Dashboard. In the "Overview" section, find the "Total Income" card. Enter your monthly income in the input field provided and it will save automatically.

3. Adding Expenses

In the "Add New Expense" section, fill out the form with the expense description, amount, category, and date. Click "Add Expense". The expense will appear in the "Your Expenses" table below.

4. Managing Expenses

In the "Your Expenses" table:

  • **View:** Expenses are listed with details. By default, they are sorted by date (newest first).
  • **Edit:** Click the "Edit" button on an expense row. The details will populate the "Add New Expense" form. Make your changes and click "Update Expense".
  • **Delete:** Click the "Delete" button on an expense row. Confirm the deletion when prompted.
  • **Filter:** Use the "Filter by Category" dropdown or the "Filter by Month" input to narrow down your expense list.

5. Viewing Summary

The "Overview" section at the top always shows your total income, total expenses, and the remaining balance, updated in real-time.

6. Downloading Data

Click the "Download Expenses as CSV" button (found below the expenses list) to save your current expense data as a CSV file.

7. Resetting Data

If you wish to clear all your income and expense data for your account, click the "Reset All User Data" button. You will be asked to confirm this action as it cannot be undone.

8. Logging Out

Click the "Logout" button in the header of the dashboard to end your session and return to the login page.

Future Considerations

  • Data visualization (charts).
  • More robust backend and database for cloud data storage and synchronization (currently client-side only).
  • Ability to define custom expense categories directly in the UI.
